Project 02- Clayes Performing Arts Center Design Project

Problem

Clayes Performing Arts Center (CPAC) is in need of a rebrand and application that addresses a few different issues

  • Student attendance is low
  • Attendees are unable to easily reserve seats online
  • The parking information and wayfinding system (environmental design) could use more improvement to provide more clarity
  • The online ticket purchasing system needs to be updated
  • Promotional presence around campus (physical space) and digital collateral (online) needs to be more exciting and engaging

Solution

New Experience (Application Solution)

  • Seat reservations through app
  • Live Parking information
  • Invite friends feature
  • Social media sharing (add new experience)
  • Featured events
  • Rating/comment system

New Experience (Environmental Solution)

  • Projections protruding through glass
  • Informational/Promotional posters
  • Environmental design & wayfinding
  • Parking navigation/signage

KeyWords

  • Playful
  • Fun
  • Elegant
  • Energetic
  • Interactive
  • Classy
  • Professional
  • Friendly
  • Futuristic
  • Whimsical

Audience

Students, alumni, friends and family of performers.

Context

The design will be seen in physical space through posters and signage, as well as the digital space of a phone application and augmented reality.

Response

The response should be that the user experience was flawless from beginning to end including the application and event experience

Message

CPAC is a one of a kind, modern performing arts center that provides a fun experience for people of all ages.
