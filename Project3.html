Project 03: Nessity Design Brief

Purpose

Provide people with products without leaving their house. It is convenient and saves time, inexpensive, more choices of household products. The consumer will never have to worry about running out of essentials. Efficient for the consumer who can be worry free, ideal for tight storage space as well as someone who may be in a budget.

Problems

  • Building an easy navigating website sell everyday items
  • Driving inconvenience
  • Environmental impact
  • Name of the logo is difficult to read/understand

Audience

The main target of this company will be primarily urban areas. The goal consumers will be college students and new families.Urban communities-which driving may be more challenging

Message

The logo should clearly represent that this is a user friendly service. The message of this design should communicate that Nessity is simple and easy to use. The service ia quick and reliable delivery.

Tagline

  • "home essentials delivered."
  • “Small space, small price, big value”
  • "Home essentials delivered"
  • "Buy bulk without the bulk"
  • "Essential life style planning"
  • "Worry-free efficient online shopping"
  • "Essentials tailored for you at your own time"
  • "Subscription service"
  • "Never forget your essesntials"
