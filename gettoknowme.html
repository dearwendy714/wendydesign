    Favorite Websites

    Pinterest

    www.pinterest.com

    Pinterest homepage is one of my go to website. It has a bookmark which allows the user to place and save and store links from all over the Web. While still maintaining visually appealing. Pinterest is a great source of inspiration as an easy source for wide range of topics. Also, a great way to promote brands giving increase traffic to website. I like while you are able to do many things you can connect with others by sharing pins. The interface of the webpage is good because it is simple, clean, and easy to use.It’s become my new “Google” looking for ideas for how to better organize my kitchen, or a suggestion for a good novel to read, or how to make my own natural products…..Pinterest is where I head FIRST. It is also a great party-planning resource. Makes planning and organizing a party easier. Making use of one of the “secret boards” that Pinterest now offers pinning ideas for inspiration in pulling off something captivating!

    Audible

    www.audible.com

    Audible is an Amazon company that produces and sells hundreds of thousands of popular audiobooks.I love listening to audiobooks. I like both audiobooks and traditional books. The audios are awesome for when doing house cleaning, yard work or other things to do besides sit and read. Some books are better to consume over audio than over text. I find that conceptual books and biographies are better off consumed over audio than text. Audiobooks are really convenient. You can have them on your phone, iPod, computer and in the car. This makes the content accessible and you can easily continue where you left off from last time. It’s especially effective when you’re in downtime. I especially like to listen to Anthony Robbins audiobooks on my way to work and I learned a lot of things while I was stuck in traffic. Plus, it’s really uplifting that motivates you to do great things while you’re learning at the same time.

    Google

    www.google.com

    It is a good place to get quick, general knowledge information without having to do a Google search. Search for information about (someone or something) on the Internet using the search engine Google. You can easily google an old friend or teacher and you can find that person current email address. Personalized search through Google is great at refining results to bring up stuff I like. Just like web search, news search remains a great performer to help me find current content. I also like Google Maps on seeing the display of the places other than seeing a map. I also use Google Translate as a translation tool. It also has a google drive which you can have files which you can store online, like photos, stories, designs, drawing, recording, and videos. The ability to has access to see your stuff anywhere regardless if it is smartphone, tablet or computer. The ability to share files ad folders makes it easy for anyone to view or download. It amazing how a google search has evolved to something more and be a strong force of resource and information.
