Design process

Rather than jumping right into the design, I save myself time and energy by first researching the topic and understanding exactly what your client needs through this step by step process

Define the Problem

You can’t find a solution until you have a clear idea of what the problem is.

Brainstorm Ideas

Group discussion to produce ideas and ways of solving problems.

Discussion/ Organize

Talk or write about (a topic) in detail, taking into account different ideas and opinions.

Sketch/ Scratch

A rough drawing representing the chief features of an object or scene and often made as a preliminary study

Critique/ Elimination

Analyzing a design, and giving feedback on whether it meets its objectives. A design critique usually manifests as a group conversation with the ultimate goal of improving a design.

Variation

Variety and Variation. Using a range of different qualities or instances of an art element to create a desired visual effect-example a variety of shapes, colors, etc. Variety can add interest and break the monotony of simple repetitions.

Test         

Test design could require all or one of: knowledge of the software, and the business area it operates on, knowledge of the functionality being tested, knowledge of testing techniques and heuristics.

Improve

Reflect on all of your feedback and decide if or to what extent it should be incorporated. It is often helpful to take solutions back through the Design Process to refine and clarify them.
